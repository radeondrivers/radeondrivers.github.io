How to download older radeon driver manually?


  1. Head to the official AMD support website, select your AMD radeon drivers card.
  2. Click on the Submit option.
  3. In the Drivers and Software section, select the link labeled Previous Drivers.
  4. Select Windows 11 - 64-Bit Edition, then the required Radeon graphics driver.
  5. Click on the download option.
  6. Unpack the downloaded setup and follow the wizard to install the older AMD Radeon graphics driver.
